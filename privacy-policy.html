Overview

HomieFix ("the App") helps you diagnose and fix home repair issues using AI-powered analysis. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Problem Descriptions: Text you enter describing your repair issue
  • Photos: Images you choose to upload for analysis (optional)
  • Home Profile: Information about your home type, age, and systems (optional, stored locally)
  • Repair History: Past analyses saved on your device
  • Email Address: If you choose to provide it for account features (optional)

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Analysis: Your descriptions and photos are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate repair recommendations
  • Personalization: Your home profile helps provide more relevant advice
  • History: Saved locally on your device for your reference

Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini API to analyze your repair issues. When you submit a problem:

  • Your description and photos are sent to Google's servers
  • Google processes this data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not permanently store your photos on our servers

Data Storage

  • Device Identifier: We generate a unique device ID to link your data across sessions
  • Server Storage: Your repair analyses are stored on our secure servers to track usage and improve our service
  • Local Storage: Your home profile is also stored locally on your device for offline access
  • Email (Optional): If you provide an email, it's stored with your account for potential future features
  • You Control Your Data: You can request deletion of your data by contacting us

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with Google Gemini for the purpose of providing AI analysis.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Use the app without providing photos or email
  • Delete your home profile from the app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Request deletion of all server-side data by emailing us
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion at any time. We may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and on this page.
